# Netherlands (NL)

## Context

The Netherlands has strong open-data infrastructure for public finance and procurement. Key agencies and portals:

- **TenderNed** — National procurement platform (`tenderned.nl`). All public tenders above EU thresholds are published here.
- **Open Spending NL** — Municipal and provincial spending data via `openspending.nl`.
- **CBR / Rijksbegroting** — National budget and financial reporting via `rijksfinancien.nl`.
- **Woo (Wet open overheid)** — Dutch freedom of information law (replaced Wob in 2022).

## Relevant legislation

- Aanbestedingswet 2012 (Procurement Act)
- Wet open overheid (Woo) — FOIA equivalent
- Gemeentewet — Municipal governance and budgets

## Focus areas

- Procurement split signals (contracts just below mandatory tender thresholds)
- Unusual single-supplier patterns in municipal procurement
- Subsidy allocation anomalies
