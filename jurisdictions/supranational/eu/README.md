# European Union (EU)

## Context

EU-level procurement and financial transparency portals:

- **TED** (Tenders Electronic Daily) — All public procurement above EU thresholds across member states.
- **Financial Transparency System (FTS)** — EU institutional spending on grants, contracts, and budget support.
- **OpenTender.eu** — Structured procurement data aggregated from TED.
- **Kohesio** — EU cohesion policy project data.

## Relevant legislation

- EU Public Procurement Directives (2014/24/EU, 2014/25/EU)
- EU Financial Regulation (2018/1046)
- Access to documents regulation (1049/2001)

## Focus areas

- Cross-border procurement patterns and nationality bias
- EU fund disbursement anomalies
- Threshold-straddling contracts across member states
